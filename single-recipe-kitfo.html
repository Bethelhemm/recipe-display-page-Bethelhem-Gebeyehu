Kitfo

Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish made from minced or finely chopped raw beef, seasoned with spices and typically served with injera (a sourdough flatbread). Kitfo can be served well-cooked, lightly cooked, or even raw depending on your preference.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Begin by thoroughly mixing the ground beef with the korerima, mitmita, and some salt; then top it all off with the butter.

step 2

Then add the mix onto a pan or skillet and heat until the meat is cooked as your liking.

step 3

Serve your Kitfo hot with Injera or with sourdough flatbread(kocho)
